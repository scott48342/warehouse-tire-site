# Wheel and Tire Packages: Why Buying Together Saves You Money

Upgrading your wheels? You'll need new tires too. While you could buy them separately, there's a smarter way: **wheel and tire packages**.

## What is a Wheel and Tire Package?

A wheel and tire package includes:
- **4 wheels** in your chosen style
- **4 tires** properly sized for those wheels
- **Mounting** - Tires professionally fitted to wheels
- **Balancing** - Weights added for smooth rotation
- **TPMS sensors** - Ready to sync with your vehicle

Everything arrives at your door **ready to bolt on**. No tire shop visit needed for mounting.

## Why Packages Save You Money

### 1. Bundle Discounts
Retailers offer better pricing when you buy together. Typical savings:
- **10-15% off** compared to buying separately
- **Free mounting and balancing** (usually $20-25 per tire)
- **Included TPMS sensors** (can cost $50-80 each)

**Example savings on a typical truck package:**

|------|----------|---------|
| 4 Wheels | $1,200 | — |
| 4 Tires | $900 | — |
| Mounting/Balance | $100 | $0 |
| TPMS Sensors | $200 | $0 |
| **Total** | **$2,400** | **$1,850** |
| **Savings** | — | **$550** |

### 2. Guaranteed Fitment
When you buy a package, the retailer ensures:
- Wheels fit your vehicle's bolt pattern and hub
- Tires are the correct size for those wheels
- Overall diameter works with your suspension

No guesswork, no returns, no headaches.

### 3. One Shipment, One Install
- Everything arrives together
- One tracking number to watch
- One install appointment
- Done in an hour instead of multiple trips

## What to Look for in a Package

### Complete Packages Include:
✅ Wheels sized for your vehicle  
✅ Tires mounted on the wheels  
✅ Computer balancing  
✅ New TPMS sensors or bands  
✅ Lug nuts (if needed)  
✅ Center caps  

### Watch Out For:
❌ "Wheel only" packages that don't include tires  
❌ Missing TPMS sensors  
❌ Unmounted packages (you pay for mounting separately)  
❌ No fitment guarantee
